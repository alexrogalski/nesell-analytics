## Hat-Specific Findings

### Dad Hats
- Unstructured, low-profile dad hats with embroidered text are the #1 print-on-demand hat format.
- Washed/garment-dyed finish (soft, vintage look) is trending for spring 2026 — aligns with Printful's offering.
- Pastel colorways (soft pink, powder blue, sage green, lavender) are the dominant color story for spring 2026.
- Embroidery has a **35% lower return rate** than printed alternatives due to higher perceived quality.
- Nesell product base confirmed: `dad_hat` and `trucker_cap` keys in `image_config.json`.

### Trucker Caps
- Retro trucker hats are explicitly named as a spring 2026 trend (mesh back, structured front).
- Strong in DE and NL markets where outdoor/sporty aesthetics are mainstream.
- Two-tone colorblocked trucker caps (e.g., Finn Two-Tone style) are gaining momentum.
- Mesh back = breathability signal = strong spring/summer positioning keyword.
- 5-year growth of +450% makes this the highest long-term growth hat category.

### Bucket Hats (not in current lineup — opportunity)
- Not in current Printful lineup for Nesell.
- Google Trends: peak at index 88 in June 2025, dramatically outperforming baseball caps in summer.
- 2026: Y2K nostalgia is driving bucket hat demand — plush textures, rich hues, festival/casual positioning.
- Printful offers bucket hats for EU fulfillment — worth evaluating for margin fit.
- **If added by April 30**, Nesell captures the May–July bucket hat peak (the strongest window).

### 2026 Trend Additions (fresh data)
- **Y2K nostalgia hats:** Backwards flat caps (from '80s hip-hop culture), logo prints, tie-dye bucket hats — Printful has officially named Y2K as a top 2026 streetwear trend for ecommerce brands.
- **Crochet/texture hats:** Fashion editors naming crochet hats a top spring 2026 trend — niche but relevant for FR/IT markets.
- **Funny/slogan embroidery:** High shareability, lower competition, strong ROAS — "Plant Dad", "Here Comes the Sun", "Grill Master" etc.

### Styles to Deprioritize for Spring
- Beanies — demand drops sharply after March.
- Wool/felt hats — early spring only, drops off quickly.
- Wide-brim fashion hats — too niche for Amazon POD marketplace.
