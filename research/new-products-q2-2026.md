## Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Alibaba quality inconsistent | Returns, bad reviews | Always order samples first; use inspection service |
| FBA storage fees on slow movers | Margin erosion | Start with 100 units, reorder based on velocity |
| Seasonal products (cooling mat) | Dead stock after summer | Launch by April, liquidate by September |
| CE/LFGB certification gaps | Listing suspension | Verify certs before ordering; use certified suppliers |
| Currency risk (USD/EUR/PLN) | Margin fluctuation | Price in EUR, source in USD, hedge with forward contracts |
| Competition entering same niches | Price pressure | Differentiate with bundles, German packaging, A+ content |
