## 2. Phase 1 Registration — Exact Steps

### Registration URL
**https://seller-eu.temu.com** (EU Seller Center)

### Required Documents Checklist

| # | Document | Where to get it | Est. time |
|---|----------|----------------|-----------|
| 1 | KRS extract (fresh, <3 months) | ekrs.ms.gov.pl | Instant (online) |
| 2 | NIP confirmation | podatki.gov.pl | Already have |
| 3 | EU VAT ID (PL prefix) | Verify at ec.europa.eu/taxation_customs/vies | Already have |
| 4 | OSS certificate | Check with tax office / e-Urząd Skarbowy | 1-2 days if not registered |
| 5 | Bank account (EUR IBAN preferred) | Your bank | Already have |
| 6 | Passport/ID scan of owner | Physical document | Ready |
| 7 | Proof of business address | Utility bill or bank statement (<180 days) | Ready |
| 8 | PL phone number | Must be active | Ready |
| 9 | Store logo (min 800x800 px) | Design or use existing | 1 day if needed |

### Registration Steps

1. Go to **https://seller-eu.temu.com** → Click "Register" / "Sign Up"
2. Select **business location: Poland** (⚠️ NON-EDITABLE after submission)
3. Choose **Semi-Managed model** (you control pricing, ship from Exportivo)
4. Upload all documents — **CRITICAL: names must match EXACTLY across all documents** (68% of delays are name mismatches)
5. Select target markets: start with **DE + PL** (highest volume)
6. Complete online identity verification (video call or automated)
7. Wait **7-12 business days** for approval (automated verification → manual KYC → final audit)
8. If stalled: email **merchandise@temu.com** as backup

### Estimated Timeline
- Document prep: **1-3 days**
- Application submission: **1 day**
- Approval: **7-12 business days**
- **Total to first listing: ~2-3 weeks**
